Cinnamon Grand Colombo

#1 Best Value of 68 5-Star Hotels in Sri Lanka


Located in Colombo, Cinnamon Grand Colombo is connected to a shopping center. Crescat Shopping Mall and Liberty Plaza are worth checking out if shopping is on the agenda, while those wishing to experience the area's natural beauty can explore Muthurajawela Visitor Centre and Galle Face Green. Traveling with kids? Consider Dehiwala Zoological Garden, or check out an event or a game at Sinhalese Sports Club. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including golfing.

Hilton

#2 Best Value of 68 5-Star Hotels in Sri Lanka


Located on a lake, Hilton Colombo is in Fort neighborhood and is connected to a shopping center. Colombo Harbor and Royal Colombo Golf Club are worth checking out if an activity is on the agenda, while those in the mood for shopping can visit Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct and One Galle Face. Traveling with kids? Consider Dehiwala Zoological Garden and Water World Lanka. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including golfing and skiing.

Shangri-La Colombo

#3 Best Value of 68 5-Star Hotels in Sri Lanka


Located in Colombo, Shangri-La Colombo is connected to a shopping center. Pettah Market and Colombo City Centre are worth checking out if shopping is on the agenda, while those wishing to experience the area's natural beauty can explore Galle Face Green and Mount Lavinia Beach. Dehiwala Zoological Garden and Water World Lanka are also worth visiting. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including outlet shopping.

The Kingsbury Colombo

#4 Best Value of 68 5-Star Hotels in Sri Lanka


The Kingsbury Colombo is located in Fort, a neighborhood in Colombo, and is near a metro station and near the beach. Bank of Ceylon Tower and Colombo World Trade Center are notable landmarks, and travelers looking to shop may want to visit Dutch Hospital Shopping Precinct and Pettah Market. Traveling with kids? Consider Dehiwala Zoological Garden, or check out an event or a game at R. Premadasa Stadium. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including golfing.
